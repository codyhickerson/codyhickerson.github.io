Once upon a time.

He Came, He Saw, He Left.

Traveling far across the galaxy using a light sail that was powered by lazers from his home planet Excaltious, Liam came in search of a rare element. The element of Scaltium. Scaltium, acording to Liam was a very powerful metal. This metal not only produced ratiation, but also it emited light. But not just average light, this light was unique because it did not give off any excess energy. It only glowed to produce a red hue. Liam was told by his counselor that if he found this rare metal he could harness the power and create a brand new element that no one has ever seen. It could end the energy crisis which his planet unfortunately suffered from. Here are a few entries from his journal which was recovered from the accident.
